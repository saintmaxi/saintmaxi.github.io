Types of Anonymice

Genesis

Anonymice Phases

Burning

10000 Genesis Mice were minted, but only 3550 are available due to the project's unique minting process. After the first 2000 were minted, the rest were only mintable by spending $CHEETH or sacrificing your own existing Mice to a burn address. These Mice became forever inaccessible and, in the case of #7767 pictured above, legends in the Mice universe. Read more about the innovative minting process in the Project Intro.


Breeding

In October 2021, Anonymice introduced the first fully on-chain breeding NFT collection ever created. The collection introduced the concept of dynamic incubator images that display a built-in countdown based on Ethereum blocks, a remarkable innovation in the realm of on-chain development. Breeding will remain ongoing until all 3550 Baby Mice have been minted.

Future Plans

Mouse Dev has consistently kept things under wraps with regard to the next phase after breeding. Utility involving parents and babies together has been confirmed, and rumors about further Mice development include mutation and evolution. Amidst the speculation, the community patiently waits for the next iteration of on-chain brilliance from the development team.
