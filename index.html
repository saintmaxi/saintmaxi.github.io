Types of Anonymice

Adult

The first collection of Anonymice. 10000 were initially available, but the unique burn-to-mint mechanic lead to the burning of all but 3550. The rest exist at an inaccessible burn address, effectively removed from supply. All adults produce 1 $CHEETH per day when staked, a utility token used in the mice ecosystem. $CHEETH uses have included minting and breeding, with more coming in future Anonymice collections.

Baby/Incubator

A collection of 3550 babies mintable by breeding two adults. It costs 50 $CHEETH to breed, and after 7 days the new baby can be revealed. Baby mice owners get the same special Discord access as adults, including the #mice-alpha channel. Lucky breeders will reveal 1 of 9 legendary babies, each with a special 1-of-1 design like the Robomouse pictured above. Future utility including parents and babies has been hinted at.
