Types of Anonymice

Genesis

The first collection of Anonymice. 10000 were initially available, but the unique burn-to-mint mechanic lead to the burning of all but 3550. The rest exist at an inaccessible burn address, effectively removed from supply. All Genesis Mice produce 1 $CHEETH per day when staked, a utility token used in the Anonymice ecosystem. $CHEETH uses have included minting and breeding, with more coming in future Anonymice collections.

Baby/Incubator

A collection of 3550 Baby Mice mintable by breeding two Genesis Mice. It costs 50 $CHEETH to breed, and after 7 days the new baby can be revealed. Baby Mice owners get the same special Discord access as Genesis Mice, including the #mice-alpha channel. Some lucky breeders will even reveal 1 of 9 legendary babies, each with a special 1-of-1 design like the Robomouse pictured above. Future utility including parents and babies has been hinted at.

Breeding

Two Genesis Mice can be bred to get a baby with traits based on its parents, with a small chance at 1 of 9 legendaries. Incubator images dynamically update to reflect the time (in Ethereum blocks) until reveal, and this rendering occurs 100% on-chain. Read more about breeding in the Breeding Primer.
